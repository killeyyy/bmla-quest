## 🎯 Exam strategy (do May 24 night, internalize)

1. **First 5 minutes:** Read ALL 7 questions before writing anything. Pick the 6 you're strongest on. Skip the worst one.
2. **Time budget:** 3 hours ÷ 6 questions = **30 min per question** (with 5 min buffer × 6 = 30 min total slack).
3. **Show every step.** Partial credit is your friend. A wrong final answer with right method usually gets 2-3 of 4 marks.
4. **Common easy wins:** triangular matrix det (= product diagonal), eigenvalues of triangular matrix (= diagonal), check Markov column sums = 1, sanity check sum of eigenvalues = trace.
5. **If stuck:** move on, come back. Don't burn 25 min on one problem when 5 others wait.
6. **Last 10 min:** check arithmetic, signs, transcription errors.
