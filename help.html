🆘 Emergency / troubleshooting

"The tracker shows 404 even after I typed the password"

The tab might've been in an input field. Click somewhere blank on the page first, then type xsurv again. Or just visit bmla-quest.vercel.app/x.html?k=xsurv directly.

"My BMLA Quest progress disappeared"

Progress is stored in browser localStorage. It vanishes if you: clear browser data, use Incognito mode, or switch browsers. There's no cloud sync. Avoid clearing site data on bmla-quest.vercel.app.

"I accidentally hit Reset"

Reset confirms before wiping in both apps. But if you confirmed and lost data, there's no undo unless you previously exported a backup (📤 button on tracker).

"I forgot how to access something"

You're on the answer page. Bookmark bmla-quest.vercel.app/help.html and you'll never lose track.
